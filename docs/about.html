About Me

Hello, I'm Brady Phillips! I am currently in my final year studying Computer Science at the University of Alabama at Birmingham (UAB). Welcome to my personal portfolio website to display my projects, resume and anything else I wish to show the world.

Background

Ever since I was a kid I always knew that I was in love with technology, and I wanted to do something in life that would allow me to learn more about technology and use it on a day-to-day basis So I found Computer Science and I have been hooked since.

My journey to learn Computer Science starts back in middle school. In middle school, I joined an organization called Technology Student Association (TSA). In TSA I did game design where I got my first introduction to coding, The game was nothing all that crazy. It was just a little ping pong game where after you scored you had to answer a question on a random topic like history, math, etc. I wish I still had that game but I have lost it, unfortunately. Even so, I was able to get second place for game design at State which was hosted at the University of Alabama.

After middle school, I did some web design in high school, just mainly HTML, not CSS or JS. After high school, I went to Coastal Alabama Community College and got my AS in Computer Science. At Coastal they did not teach much, they just taught C++ basics and object orientation. My coding world expanded dramatically once I transferred to the University of Alabama at Birmingham (UAB). Since attending UAB I have learned Python, C, CSS, JS, Databases, AWS, GIT, and even Racket!
